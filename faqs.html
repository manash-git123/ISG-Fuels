F.A.Q

  • Where all are you operating from currently?

    We deliver all across Pune at the moment and hope to expand in Maharashtra and other states too.

  • What are your working hours?

    We are currently functional between 9am-6pm, Monday - Saturday

  • Where do you source your diesel from?

    All our fuel is sourced from HPCL RO, Pune.

  • Do you deliver petrol and CNG?

    At the moment we are only delivering Diesel. However we seek a vast change in the industry by providing CNG or/and petrol in the future if permitted by the government.

  • Is there a cost difference in filling fuel through Nozzle Express and at an actual fuel pump?

    There is a nominal transport charge plus GST applicable on the destination and quantity to be delivered.

  • Do you deliver to car/bus/truck fleets?

    We will be delivering to fleets in the near future in a compounded yard, if the Government permits. However, at the moment we are catering to the construction industry. You could check out our Services page for more.

  • Can we pre-schedule an order?

    Yes, you can do that on the app.

  • What if there is no fuel delivery available at the time I need it?

    All orders need to be raised a minimum of one day prior to the expected delivery date. In the unusual situation that a vehicle breaks down or something unexpected happens and we are unable to deliver to you on the day desired, it shall be done immediately on the next working day.

  • What is the lead time required for placing an order? / When is the latest that I can place an order by?

    In order to deliver the hassle-free experience promised, all orders are required to be placed at least one day prior to the date of expected delivery.

  • Am I allowed to cancel my order?

    Orders once accepted, will not be cancelled.

  • What payment options are available?

    You can pay via Credit Card, Cheque, NEFT, RTGS, Paytm Google Pay or through Cash on Delivery as well.

  • Is Cash on Delivery available as a payment option?

    Yes. You must choose that option while placing the order on the app.

  • Is there a method of testing the quality of the diesel delivered to us?

    Yes. We have a density and 5ltr Quantity check in the tanker at any given point of time.

  • How do we monitor the fuel consumption patterns through the app?

    You can access your order history and an asset-wise track report is also available on the App.
