SERVICES

Focused on solving your refueling hassles at ground level, our on-demand, online fuel delivery system to your doorstep is the one stop solution you need. On downloading the app, you can fill in all your requirements and enjoy the convenience of accurate, timely, safe and reliable diesel delivery at your doorstep. Currently, we are catering to:

  • Generator Refueling
  • Industrial Refueling
  • Fleet refueling

    • Generator Refueling

    Large parts of India even today face the trouble of power cuts. While some have learnt to adjust and accept, entities like hospitals, manufacturing units, commercial complexes, hotels, malls, tech-parks and more cannot afford to wait for the lights to come back, and hence have adapted to generator backup systems.
    Using Nozzle Express to refuel these generators ensures:
  • Timely delivery of the fuel at your location without the hassle of transport and storage facilities
  • Elimination of messy cleanup processes due to spilling of fuel during the transfer
  • A more concrete monitoring process of fuel consumption for audit and budgeting purposes
    •

    Industrial Refueling

    Our construction industry relies heavily on the availability of diesel for running their machines. Seeing workers idle on site awaiting machines like the RCC machine, earthmovers, bulldozers, excavators etc. is a common site in India, and most of the time these delays occur due to the unprecedented stops for refueling.
    Nozzle Express helps ensure timely delivery of your projects by eliminating these hurdles by:
  • 1.Cutting down the time loss waiting for equipment to go and get fueled again
  • 2.Cutting down the need for storing the fuel on site
  • 3.Monitoring the consumption of fuel for accounting purposes and to ensure there is no theft in the processes along the way
    •

    Fleet Refueling

    With e-commerce and other emerging forms of business models on the rise, the need for transportation of goods and more across the country has also seen a huge rise to meet the demand. Companies have begun investing in fleets according to their requirement. However, with that, comes the maintenance of running it smoothly. One of the biggest headaches fleet owners have is that of refueling all their vehicles at once.
    With Nozzle Express, you not only save time and effort to get the job done, but also see a change in the bottom lines of your accounting books:
  • Save on dead mileage going to and from the petrol pumps
  • Get your vehicles filled at the time they are not being used so as to save idle time
  • Ensure you keep a check on the allowance for fuel given to your employees and at the same time eliminate the risk of theft
