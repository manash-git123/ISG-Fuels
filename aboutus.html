ABOUT US

ISG Fuels Pvt. Ltd. is a team of individuals focused on solving everyday problems with the simplest solutions. Having been in the Petrol Pump industry since 1978, Mr.Iqbal Singh Guruditta was a silent observer to the stressful routine of refueling large quantities of diesel for over 12 years! Knowing that there had to be an easier way to accomplish a recurring activity like this, he & Mr.Inderjeet Singh Guruditta dived into understanding every detail that goes into the process in the early 2000s.

In 2008, they successfully implemented a convenient, efficient and revolutionary solution of ordering Diesel from the comfort of your home or office.

They then deliver your diesel to you when you want and where you want it, so you can use your valuable time for more important things.

After all, time is money. And we help you save both.

HOW do we work?

A pioneer in the field, our W&M calibrated, PESO approved, certified fleet of tankers deliver all your diesel requirements to your doorstep in the safest, most accurate and convenient manner possible complete with nozzle and meter readings.

All you need to do is install the App and place your order. After that, dead mileage, machine downtime, fuel pilferage, and fuel adulteration will be things of the past.

Currently we are catering to requirements of 200ltrs and above which makes us your go-to-solution if you are running a hospital, institute, residential or commercial complex with generator backup requirements; or if you own a fleet of buses, trucks and other vehicles which need to be fed regularly.
